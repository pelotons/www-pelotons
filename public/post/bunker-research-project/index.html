The Bunker Research Project

An ongoing series of short-films riding to the World War II bunkers in the Alps, explored in the 1960’s by the Rough Stuff Fellowship and documented on the Bunker Research site and accompanying book.

The plan is to ride to each bunker or a series of bunkers documenting the route, terrain and the bunker with hopefully some history of the role the bunker played in the war.

Starting Spring 2023
